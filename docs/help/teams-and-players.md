# Teams & Players

## Adding Teams

1. From the dashboard, click **Teams**
2. Click **New Team**
3. Enter the team name
4. Upload a logo (optional — square images work best, 200x200px minimum)
5. Save

### Team Limits
- **Basic plan**: Limited number of teams (configurable)
- **Pro plan**: Unlimited teams

## Managing Teams

From the Teams list, you can:
- **View** a team's details, players, and match history
- **Edit** a team's name or logo
- See all tournaments and groups the team is assigned to

## Adding Players

1. From the dashboard, click **Add Player**
2. Enter the player's name and details
3. Assign them to a team
4. Save

Players appear on:
- The team detail page on your public site
- Match lineups (when added via the Formation Editor)
- Player statistics views

## Player Statistics

Stats are tracked per match and aggregated automatically:
- **Goals** scored
- **Assists**
- **Yellow cards**
- **Red cards**
- **Minutes played**

View aggregated stats from **Player Stats** in the dashboard.

Stats update when match events are recorded. To correct stats, update the match event data.

## Tips

- Create all teams before setting up tournament groups
- Teams are organization-scoped — they can be reused across tournaments
- Upload clear, high-contrast logos for best display at small sizes
