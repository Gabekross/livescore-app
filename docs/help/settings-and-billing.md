# Site Settings & Billing

## Site Settings

Access settings from **Settings** in the admin dashboard or the top nav bar.

### Branding
- **Site Name**: Displayed in the navigation bar and browser tab
- **Site Logo**: Shown alongside the site name (square image recommended)
- **Description**: Used for SEO meta tags and social sharing

### Theme
Choose a visual theme for your public site:
- **UEFA Dark**: Premium dark sports look (default)
- **UEFA Light**: Clean, light variant with green/yellow accent

Theme changes apply immediately to your public site.

### SEO
- Set custom page titles and descriptions
- Configure Open Graph metadata for social sharing
- Each page generates its own SEO metadata automatically

## Billing & Plans

### Basic Plan (Free Trial)
Your organization starts with a free trial including:
- Core features (tournaments, live scores, standings)
- Limited number of teams
- Basic branding options

### Pro Plan
Upgrade for full access:
- Unlimited teams
- News publishing
- Media library
- Match operator accounts
- Advanced branding and settings
- Priority support

### Billing Options
- **Weekly**: Flexible, short-term
- **Monthly**: Most popular
- **Yearly**: Best value (significant savings)

### Managing Your Subscription
1. Go to **Settings** in the admin dashboard
2. Find the **Billing** section
3. Click to manage your subscription, upgrade, or change plans
4. Billing is handled securely through Stripe

### Trial Expiration
When your trial ends:
- Your public site remains accessible to visitors
- Admin features become limited
- Upgrade to Pro to restore full access
- Go to Settings to choose a plan

## Sponsors

Manage sponsors from the **Sponsors** page:
1. Add sponsor logos and links
2. Sponsors appear in a rotating strip on your public site
3. Great for local business partnerships
