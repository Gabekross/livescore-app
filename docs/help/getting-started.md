# Getting Started with KoluSports

## What is KoluSports?

KoluSports is a multi-organization football platform that lets you run your own sports website with live scores, tournaments, teams, and news. Each organization gets its own subdomain and fully customizable site.

## Quick Start (5 minutes)

### 1. Sign Up
Visit the platform and create an account. You'll start with a free trial that gives you access to all core features.

### 2. Access Your Dashboard
After signing in, you'll land on the admin dashboard — your command center for managing everything.

### 3. Create Your First Tournament
- Click **Tournaments** in the Operations section
- Click **New Tournament** and give it a name
- Add **stages** (e.g., Group Stage, Knockout)
- Create **groups** within each stage
- **Assign teams** to groups

### 4. Add Teams
- Click **Teams** from the dashboard
- Click **New Team** to register teams
- Upload a team logo (optional)
- Teams can be assigned to any tournament group

### 5. Schedule Matches
- Navigate to a tournament group
- Click **New Match** to schedule fixtures
- Set the date, time, and teams
- Matches appear on your public site automatically

### 6. Go Live on Match Day
- Open a scheduled match
- Update scores and events in real time
- Your public site updates instantly

## Your Public Site

Your organization gets a dedicated subdomain (e.g., `yourleague.kolusports.com`). Visitors can:

- View live, upcoming, and completed matches
- Browse tournament standings and fixtures
- See team rosters and details
- Read news articles (Pro plan)
- Experience your custom branding and theme
